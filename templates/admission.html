Admissions

Thank you for your interest in applying to My College. Below are the steps and requirements for the admissions process:

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit all required documents, including transcripts, test scores, and recommendation letters.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend an interview (if required).
  5. Receive admission decision.

Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Standardized test scores (e.g., SAT, ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Additional requirements for specific programs (if applicable)

Orientation/Enrollment: 05-08-2024
